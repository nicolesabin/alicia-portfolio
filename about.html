About

I'm Alicia

My design philosophy is simple: create work that conveys the intended message through a visual system that grabs the audience's attention and engages them to look further.

My Approach

I solve design problems through concept and aesthetics, with aesthetics being secondary. A trendy design piece with no concept fails. I get to concept through word associations, mind-mapping, and mood boards. The mood board is where I combine concept with aesthetic value, defining the look and feel of a design.

My Style

I'm a detail-oriented designer. My design solutions are whimsical and playful, backed by strong hierarchy, typography, and alignments. Every element is carefully considered to create cohesive and impactful designs.

My Inspiration

Inspiration strikes me like lightning or bubbles like a daydream. I'm inspired by everything around me: a beautiful color palette, interesting structure, or the feeling of a grey rainy day. I find beauty and design opportunities in the everyday world around me.

My Process

My process involves starting with rough ideas and then refining. I use word associations, mind-mapping, and mood boards to develop concepts. I combine these concepts with aesthetic value to define the look and feel of each design. The result is work that is both conceptually strong and visually compelling.
