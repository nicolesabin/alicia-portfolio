Design Philosophy

Concept First

I solve design problems through concept and aesthetics, with aesthetics being secondary. A trendy design piece with no concept fails.

Detail-Oriented

My design solutions are whimsical and playful, backed by strong hierarchy, typography, and alignments.

Inspired by Everything

Inspiration strikes me like lightning or bubbles like a daydream. I'm inspired by everything around me: a beautiful color palette, interesting structure, or the feeling of a grey rainy day.
